    Diversity analysis of DNA metabarcoding data

    By Antton Alberdi & Ostaizka Aizpurua, University of Copenhagen.

    Latest update: 12th April 2020

    Welcome to the Diversity analysis of DNA metabarcoding data electronic book. In this website you will find essential information to understand and perform diversity analyses on different kind of biological systems characterised using DNA metabarcoding or targeted amplicon sequencing approaches. This website is meant to be an open resource for all students and researchers working on diversity analysis of DNA metabarcoding data.
